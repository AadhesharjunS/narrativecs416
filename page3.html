Electricity Generation by GDP Per Capita

(Filter by Country)

Given its usefulness and importance in the modern era, electricity can certainly be attributed to something that advanced humanity beyond the Industrial Revolution and gave us technologies that were once inconceivable. This certainly contributed to the increase in quality of living and also raised the cost of living. While each country has had a different rate of increase through he years, with some slow while others quick, the increase in wellbeing is inevitable as seen through the GDP Per Capita measure.
