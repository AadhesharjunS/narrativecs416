Electricity Generation Over CO2 Emisions

(Filter by Country)

As seen in the dataset from ourworldindata.org, carbon dioxide levels can come from a variety of sources, and it has a very profound effect on humanity in terms of ailments. Many of these sources of electricity power plants of varying types. Though this part of the data was quite split where the electricity generated was sometimes a positive correlation and sometimes had no correlation, the relationship between the two was still clear. While electricity production may not be too significant of an issue to focus on for lowering CO2 levels, it is certainly something that can radically alter the levels by cutting down on dirty supplies like fossil fuels and switching to things like renewable energy and making nuclear power sources even more common.
