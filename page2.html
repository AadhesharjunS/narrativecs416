Electricity Generation Over Year

(Filter by Country)

Electricity is often taken for granted nowadays in developed countries; we can turn it on with the flick of a switch. However, in many other countries, the infrastructure for continuous electricity is simply not viable. That said, electricity has been getting cleaner and more accessible throughout the years, and it has been on a steady increase for decades. Feel free to look through nearly 196 countries and see how their electricity generation increased over the years.
